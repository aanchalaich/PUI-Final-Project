Financials Page

The Financials Page displays the three financial statements (Balance Sheet, Income Statement, Cash Flow) from previous years. These statements are very comprehensive and provide a detailed look at each of the line items for the statements.

    The Financials section will help you in your stock research by:
  • Displayinf full financial statements for current and previous fiscal years and quarters.
  • Every line item is displayed for full detail and transparency

Amazon's 2019 annual EPS was $23.50, a 13.5% increase from the previous year. This is tremendous growth for the company, solidifying Amazon's stance as a growth stock.
