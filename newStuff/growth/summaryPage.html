Summary Page

The "Summary tab" gives you a quick look at key info like the fund name, price, and return. This section gives you a glance at the most current statistics of a company. These will allow you to quickly assess how a company is performing right now.

    The Summary section will help you in your stock research by:
  • Highlighting the stock price that is currently being traded
  • Highlighting the most important ratios such as PE and EPS
  • Providing a graph to help you visualize performance over an extended period of time

Click on a button to learn more about the features of this page!

You can see from the Summary Page that Amazon has a very high PE ratio of 90+. Generally, the lower the PE ratio the more undervalued a stock is and the better of a buy it could be. However, Amazon is expected to have much higher growth than companies like Apply or Microsoft, so its PE ratio is actually a good signal. This is one reason why it is considered a "growth" company.
