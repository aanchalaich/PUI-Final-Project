Statistics Page

The Statistics page on Yahoo finance is broken down into Valuation Measures, Financial Highlights, and Trading Information. This page provides important valuation metrics from current and previous quarters and years, providing a more comprehehnsive look at a company's financial history.

    The Statistics section will help you in your stock research by:
  • Providing financial information for the company from recent history
  • Highlighting a greater variety of metrics, line items and ratios than the summary page
  • Making data available on a yearly, quarterly, and monthly basis

Click on a button to learn more about the features of this page!

The PEG ratio measures the relationship between the price/earnings ratio and earnings growth. The PEG ratio provides a more complete picture of whether a stock's price is overvalued or undervalued by analyzing both today's earnings and the expected growth rate. Amazon's PEG ratio (5 yr expected) is 1.23. For a growth company like Amazon, a 1.23 ratio is actually good as it projects good growth for the company going forward.
