Profile Page

The first thing you should do when you land on the Yahoo Finance website is to go to the "Profile" section. Going to this section first will give you the basic information you need to know about the company before you go into the financial analysis. You need to know what the company is before you know about its stock information!

    The Profile section will help you in your stock research by:

  • Telling you what the company does

  • Categorizing it into industries and sectors

  • Telling you how their board of directors is structured and how senior leadership gets compensated

Click on a button to learn more about the features of this page!
